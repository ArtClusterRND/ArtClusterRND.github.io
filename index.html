About

R+N+D is a liquid art-cluster and lab for sound-design, media-synthesis and experimental culturology that occasionally unites Ukrainian media-artists, DJs, photographers, promoters and cultural managers. Originally established in Lugansk art-cluster R+N+D from 2011 to 2014 was platform for wide range of collabaration between artists from Donbass.
